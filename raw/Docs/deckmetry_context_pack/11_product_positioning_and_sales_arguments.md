# 11 — Product Positioning and Sales Arguments

## Core positioning

Deckmetry is a supplier-side deck sales and ordering platform that turns project inputs into material takeoffs, pricing, lead capture, delivery requests, and order workflow.

## One-liner

> Deckmetry helps building suppliers turn deck ideas into organized material orders — faster for contractors, clearer for homeowners, and easier for sales teams to manage.

## Message for Wehrung's directors

Deckmetry can create a new digital sales channel for Wehrung's while keeping the sales team in control.

## Message for Wehrung's IT

Deckmetry can start without deep ERP integration. The pilot can use structured exports and admin review while IT evaluates Epicor integration options.

## Message for Wehrung's sales team

Deckmetry gives you cleaner project information earlier, so you spend less time chasing missing details and more time closing material orders.

## Message for contractors

Build a deck takeoff in minutes, see your pricing, request delivery, and track the order without relying on back-and-forth emails.

## Message for homeowners

Explore deck materials, get a material list, understand estimated cost, and request professional installation pricing.

## Why it can increase sales for Wehrung's

### Homeowners become leads
Many homeowners browse before they speak to a contractor. Deckmetry turns that browsing into a captured lead.

### Contractors become more loyal
If the portal saves contractors time, they have a stronger reason to continue buying from Wehrung's.

### Faster ordering means fewer lost orders
When ordering is slow or manual, contractors may send the list to another supplier. A faster workflow helps Wehrung's stay top-of-mind.

### Structured data improves follow-up
Wehrung's can follow up based on real project details, not vague inquiries.

### It makes Wehrung's feel more modern
A supplier with digital tools stands out against competitors who still rely only on phone/email.

## Important strategic language

Avoid saying:
- “This will replace your sales team.”
- “This automatically creates final orders.”
- “This guarantees exact pricing.”
- “This requires full Epicor integration immediately.”

Say instead:
- “This supports your sales team.”
- “This creates structured requests for review.”
- “Pricing and availability remain subject to confirmation.”
- “Epicor integration can be phased after pilot validation.”

## Strategic moat

Deckmetry becomes stronger over time if it builds:
- Supplier-specific product catalogs.
- Contractor-specific pricing.
- Repeat contractor workflows.
- Historical project data.
- Accurate BOM rules.
- ERP/catalog integrations.
- Homeowner lead channels.
