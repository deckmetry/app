# RDI Finyl Line

## Overview

RDI Finyl Line is a vinyl railing system product line referenced in the Deckmetry context pack as a known user/contractor preference. The specific configuration cited is white posts and rails with black round balusters — a common aesthetic in residential deck railing.

## Key Facts

- **Manufacturer**: RDI (Railing Dynamics Inc., a subsidiary of Associated Materials)
- **Product line**: Finyl Line — vinyl railing systems
- **Common configuration cited in Deckmetry context**: White color, black round balusters
- **Railing type**: Vinyl
- **Kit format**: Available in 6 ft and 8 ft level rail kits, stair rail kits
- **Typical railing height**: ~36"–39" depending on deck height
- **Also noted**: ADA aluminum handrails can be referenced as a graspable rail option mounted to RDI-style deck railing
