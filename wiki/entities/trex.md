# Trex

## Overview

Trex Company, Inc. is the world's largest manufacturer of composite decking and railing products. In Deckmetry's catalog, Trex is the primary brand with the most extensive collection lineup.

## Key Facts

- **Collections in Deckmetry catalog (5):**
  - **Enhance Naturals** — 5.5" x 0.94", 6 colors (Toasted Sand, Cinnamon Cove, Rocky Harbour, Foggy Wharf, Honey Grove, Golden Hour)
  - **Select** — 5.5" x 0.82", 2 colors (Whiskey Barrel, Millstone)
  - **Transcend** — 5.5" x 0.94", 6 colors (Rope Swing, Havana Gold, Lava Rock, Tiki Torch, Spiced Rum, Island Mist)
  - **Transcend Lineage** — 5.5" x 0.94", 7 colors (Rainier, Carmel, Weathered Teak, Biscayne, Pebble Beach, Jasper, Ember)
  - **Signature** — 5.5" x 1.0", 2 colors (Whidbey, Ocotillo)
- **Total colors:** 23
- **Board lengths:** 12', 16', 20' (grooved); 20' (solid)
- **Fascia options:** 1x8x12 and 1x12x12
- **Standard board width:** 5.5" face width across all collections

## Strategic Relevance

Trex is the market leader and the default brand in Deckmetry's wizard (initial state defaults to `trex` / `trex-enhance` / `Honey Grove`). Supporting Trex comprehensively is table stakes — any deck estimator tool must handle Trex products accurately. Trex's premium collections (Transcend Lineage, Signature) represent upsell opportunities for contractors using Deckmetry.
