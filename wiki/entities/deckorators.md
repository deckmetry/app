# Deckorators

## Overview

Deckorators is a composite decking brand offering value-to-mid-tier products. In Deckmetry's catalog, Deckorators is the third brand with 3 collections.

## Key Facts

- **Collections in Deckmetry catalog (3):**
  - **Venture** — 5.5" x 0.94", 3 colors (Saltwater, Sandbar, Shoreline)
  - **Voyage** — 5.5" x 0.94", 6 colors (Costa, Sierra, Khaya, Tundra, Sedona, Mesa)
  - **Summit** — 5.5" x 0.94", 3 colors (Glacier, Boulder, Cliffside)
- **Total colors:** 12
- **Board lengths:** 12', 16', 20' (grooved); 20' (solid)
- **Fascia options:** 1x12x12 only
- **Board dimensions:** Consistent 5.5" x 0.94" across all collections

## Strategic Relevance

Deckorators offers the most uniform product line in the catalog — all collections share identical board dimensions and thickness. This simplifies BOM calculations. Their Voyage collection has the broadest color range (6 colors), positioned as the mid-tier workhorse competing with Trex Enhance Naturals.
